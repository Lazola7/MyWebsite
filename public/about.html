About Phakathi Industrial Solutions (PISO)

Company Overview

Phakathi Solutions is a black-owned, B-BBEE Level 1 industrial technology provider committed to transforming industries across Africa and beyond. Guided by our mission to revolutionise industrial operations through innovative, data-driven solutions, we integrate state-of-the-art fabrication services with cutting-edge digital technologies. Our comprehensive offerings cover the entire project lifecycle—from design to implementation and ongoing support—so clients can optimise performance, enhance safety, and achieve measurable returns on investment.

Our Fabrication Workshop is at the core of our capabilities. It provides laser and plasma profiling, vessel and tank manufacturing, precision machining, custom design, heat treatment (both on-site and oven-based), rolling, and bending. By combining these advanced fabrication services with our expertise in the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), data analytics, and instrumentation and control systems, we help organisations address today's most complex operational challenges.

In addition, Phakathi Solutions is at the forefront of deploying Artificial Intelligence at the edge, allowing data to be processed directly where it is generated. This approach reduces latency, ensures greater reliability, and provides real-time insights for faster decision-making and predictive maintenance. Whether optimising fabrication workflows or enhancing on-site safety, edge-focused AI empowers our clients to remain agile while harnessing the power of industrial data.

Our Vision

To be Africa's foremost industrial partner, recognised for uniting advanced fabrication capabilities with cutting-edge digital solutions that elevate efficiency, sustainability, and community impact. We envision forging a future of shared prosperity by setting new industry benchmarks in innovation, inclusivity, and black economic empowerment.
